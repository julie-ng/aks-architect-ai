---
title: Organization Type
designer:
  title: What type of organization are you?
  description: |
    Determines how much autonomy your team has over architecture decisions. In enterprises and regulated industries, many decisions are dictated by central governance, compliance, or platform teams.
  question_type: radio
  answers:
  - key: startup
    label: Startup / Independent Team
    description: |
      Your team has full control over architecture decisions. You choose your own tools, configurations, and trade-offs without external governance constraints.
    implications:
      - design-autonomy: full
    waf_impact:
      operations: -5
      cost: -3

  - key: enterprise
    label: Enterprise
    description: |
      Some or most architecture decisions are guided by central platform teams, governance policies, or organizational standards. Your team operates within predefined guardrails.
    implications:
      - design-autonomy: limited
      - governance: organizational-standards
    waf_impact:
      security: 5
      operations: 5
      cost: 3

  - key: regulated
    label: Regulated Industry
    description: |
      Architecture decisions are heavily constrained by compliance frameworks (e.g. PCI DSS, HIPAA, FedRAMP). Most security and networking choices are dictated by regulatory requirements.
    implications:
      - design-autonomy: minimal
      - governance: compliance-mandated
    waf_impact:
      security: 10
      operations: 8
      cost: 5
---
