---
title: Organization Type
spec:
  question: Which best describes your organization?
  title: Organization Type
  description: |
    Think about how much autonomy your team has over architecture decisions. 
  question_type: radio
  answers:
  - key: startup
    label: Startup / Independent Team
    description: |
      Your team has **full control over architecture decisions**. You choose your own tools, configurations, and trade-offs.
    implications:
      - design-autonomy: full
    waf_baseline:
      operations: -5
      cost: -3

  - key: enterprise
    label: Enterprise
    description: |
      **Some** of your architecture decisions are guided by central teams, policies, or organizational standards.
    implications:
      - design-autonomy: limited
      - governance: organizational-standards
    waf_baseline:
      security: 5
      operations: 5
      cost: 3

  - key: regulated
    label: Regulated Industry
    description: |
      Architecture decisions are **heavily constrained** by compliance frameworks (e.g. PCI DSS, HIPAA, FedRAMP) and organizational standards.
    implications:
      - design-autonomy: minimal
      - governance: compliance-mandated
    waf_baseline:
      security: 10
      operations: 8
      cost: 5
---
