---
title: Team Role
designer:
  title: Which best describes your team's role?
  description: |
    Determines the scope of guidance. Cluster operators need infrastructure-level recommendations. Application developers need workload-focused guidance.
  question_type: radio
  answers:
  - key: cluster-operator
    label: Cluster Operator / Platform Team
    description: |
      Your team runs the cluster as a platform for other teams to deploy on. Responsible for infrastructure, networking, security policies, and cluster lifecycle.
    implications:
      - scope: infrastructure
      - tenancy: likely-multi-tenant
    waf_impact:
      security: 5
      operations: 8

  - key: app-developer
    label: Application Developer
    description: |
      Your team focuses on building and deploying applications. You consume the cluster as a platform and primarily care about deploying workloads, not managing infrastructure.
    implications:
      - scope: workloads
      - tenancy: likely-single-tenant
    waf_impact:
      operations: -5
